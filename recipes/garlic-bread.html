Garlic Bread

Description

Garlic bread is a delicious accessory to any meal or even a delicious meal on it's own! This recipe will walk you through the steps to create this savory dish at home and impress all the garlic lovers you are cooking for. You'll only need a few simple ingredients and about 30 minutes to create this cheesy, savory masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick of butter
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 4-5 basil leaves
  • Bread-Choose whichever you like, though a good stiff bread will produce better results:sourdough/french bread/ciabatta/francese rolls/etc
  • 1 block of parmersan cheese

Steps

  1. Let the butter sit at room temperature long enough to become soft
  2. Peel and finely chop the garlic
  3. Chop approximately 1/2 cup of parsley
  4. Combine butter, garlic, and parsley together in a bowl
  5. Mix thoroughly with a fork or whisk
  6. Preheat toaster oven to 425 F
  7. Spread garlic butter evenly onto bread
  8. Cook garlic bread in heated oven for about 10 minutes or until the garlic just starts to brown
  9. Remove bread from toaster and grate a thick layer of fresh parmesan cheese onto all pieces
  10. Put back into oven until cheese has melted all the way-Be careful not to crisp or burn the cheese
  11. Coarsely chop basil while bread is cooking
  12. Remove cheesey garlic bread from oven and sprinkle chopped basil on top of the cheese
  13. Voila! Garlic bread is ready to be enjoyed by all
